Privacy Policy.

How we protect and manage your calculations data here at bmicalculat.com.

Our commitment to your privacy.

At bmicalculat.com, your health data privacy is our absolute priority. We design our web applications to guarantee you complete control over your health metrics without server tracking risks.

1. Fully local browser computations.

Every single calculation run on our online bmi calculator is executed locally inside your web browser using client-side JavaScript. None of your parameters (age, height, weight, gender, activity profile) are ever uploaded, logged, or processed on backend cloud servers.

2. Local Storage history logs.

Our "Calculation History" tool uses browser localStorage to store calculations directly on your local drive. This data is private and unique to your browser. You can clear this log at any time by clicking the "Clear History" button on the calculator screen or clearing your browser cookies.

3. Zero clinical profile tracking.

We do not collect or purchase medical, demographic, or clinical information. We do not sell user-targeted behavioral advertisements or bundle advertising cookies that link back to your BMI inquiries.

4. Web analytics metrics.

To monitor global loading speeds and system performance, we may capture anonymous, non-personally identifiable server log statistics (like general browser types or page loading times). This is never joined with your calculation variables.

5. Policy updates.

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy. Any updates will be published immediately on this page. By continuing to use our application, you consent to our entirely local browser-based health calculation architecture.
