About bmicalculat.com.

A design-forward developer tool engineering accurate clinical health calculations.

Our mission.

bmicalculat.com was created to bridge the gap between complex health data metrics and sleek, premium digital user interfaces. We believe health utilities should be beautifully responsive, privacy-respecting, and free from intrusive trackers or ads.

Clinical accuracy.

All calculations on our platform are verified against official clinical guidelines from the World Health Organization (WHO) and the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC). We support standard metric formulas as well as standard US imperial scales to give users precise health metrics.

Design principles.

  • Stark Aesthetics: Clean borders, subtle shadows, and a typography-first layout modeled after high-end developer consoles.
  • Client-Side Engine: We do not log your weight metrics. 100% of calculations occur client-side on your device.
  • State-of-the-Art UX: Smooth slider controls, real-time calculations, dynamic speedometer indicators, and integrated local calculation history logs.

Who we are.

We are a small team of developers and health enthusiasts passionate about building premium, ad-free web software. By keeping our codebase minimal and fast, we provide one of the fastest and most accurate BMI tools on the web.
